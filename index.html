Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buildfree.io.

How does resume tailoring work?

Our resume tailoring tool analyzes the job description you provide and your existing resume to highlight relevant skills and experiences. It rearranges sections, adjusts wording, and optimizes for applicant tracking systems (ATS) to increase your chances of getting an interview.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll maintain access to your subscription features until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. Our systems are regularly audited for security compliance.

How does email integration work?

Our email integration connects securely to your email account through OAuth (no passwords stored). It scans for emails from companies you've applied to and automatically categorizes them as application confirmations, rejections, interview requests, etc. All this happens while keeping your data private and secure.

What file formats are supported for resume upload?

We support all major document formats including PDF, DOCX, DOC, and TXT. For best results, we recommend using PDF or DOCX formats as they preserve formatting better. You can also create a resume directly on our platform using our template builder.
